## What it confirms — the session model

- **Line 4 = the session, station-driven and *held*** (not a brief pulse): steady 9.20 V for ~9.7 s,
  gong riding the front. The earlier "line 4 only pulses ~1.5 s" was the partial-read artifact of
  `ring-…180939`; read whole, line 4 holds.
- **The hold is presence-gated.** The station only sustains line 4 while it senses the handset
  answering (the WF26 coil drawing ~37 mA). With P4 floating (no coil load) the station sends only
  a brief (~0.4–1 s) initiation kick and drops — no session. (See `session-…102532`, the floating-P4
  capture, and `other-station-…132858`.)
- **The door-open terminates the session — and it's the *station* dropping line 4, not a P2 seal-in.**
  At 159.25 s line 4 collapses to 0 *while P2 only sags to 7.2 V* — above where a 12 V coil releases
  (~3–5 V). If P2 held the coil, 7.2 V would keep K1 latched; it doesn't. So what's removed is the
  station's line-4 drive: the station senses the ÖT short on P2↔P3, fires the opener, and hangs up.
- **P2 is the listen tap**, tied to line 4 via K1 during the call (tracks ~9.3 V), and is also the
  ÖT line the door-open shorts to P3.
- **Gong:** 1010 / 3032 Hz, matching the other captures (the ~1010 / 841 / 672 Hz 3-Klang).

## Notes

- **IN_P4↔P4 was bridged** for this run, so CH1 is line 4 itself (no separate P4 probe needed).
- The door-open landed ~9.7 s after the gong onset (the board's 1.75 s door-open delay may pad the
