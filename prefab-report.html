Abs-max sweep on every IC pin / TX path warning

Issue ES8311 OUTP (pin 12) is the worst-margin IC pin. On every normal K1 (PTT) make, the +12 V (up to +17 V) P2 step couples through C14 back into OUTP; R26(2.2k) limits the current into the codec's AVDD ESD clamp to ~3.8 mA for a ~2.2 ms transient (tau=R26*C14), but the pin momentarily nicks just past the +3.6 V abs-max — i.e. OUTP relies on the internal ESD clamp on every talk-start, not only under fault.
